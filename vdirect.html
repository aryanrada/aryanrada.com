Video Streaming Service(VDirect)

About Project

This system is about uploading and streaming videos and movies on an online platform that users can access anywhere and at any time. They can access their favorite movies or videos at any location using their browser and the internet. Users must pay the subscription fee to access the site because the movies and videos are being uploaded by the company that spends on making them so they could earn from the views and ads users are seeing and subscribing for.

This site is constructed using Apache Server and all the user interfaces have been designed using the PHP Laravel technologies. The database connectivity is planned to use the “MySQL Connections” methodology. The standards of security and data protective mechanisms have been given an extensive choice for proper usage.

User and Role description

User:

The user's role is to stream movies and videos which is subscribed by them.

Company:

The company's role is to upload movies and videos they have made for users to see, and they can earn revenue from the views and subscriptions of users.

Admin:

The admin role is to manage the videos uploaded by the company and review how many users are in the system.
